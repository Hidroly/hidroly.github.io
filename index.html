Hidroly

Open-source, distraction-free, and designed to make drinking water simple.

Features

Private by default

Your hydration data stays on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no cloud required. Your privacy matters.

Minimalist

A clean and distraction-free interface designed to help you focus on what matters: drinking water and staying healthy.

Smart Reminders

Smart reminders that adapt to your schedule, automatically pausing while you sleep.

Summary

Get quick insights on your hydration: view your current streak, best streak, average intake and weekly chart. All in one clean view.
